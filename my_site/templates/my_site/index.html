About

I am Emmanuel Oteng, the founder of Newman Studio. With over four years of experience in digital creation, my expertise spans graphic design, digital art, photography, retouching, and clothing design. Creativity is at the heart of everything I do, and I am driven by a passion for visual storytelling. My proficiency in color theory, composition, lighting, and advanced photo manipulation enables me to craft designs that are both impactful and meaningful.

Graphic Designer & Photographer.

Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of collaborating with some of Ghana’s most influential figures, including esteemed statesmen, prominent musicians, and leading media personalities. I have designed striking visuals for music projects, including album cover art that aligns with both the sound and the spirit of the music. Additionally, I create innovative clothing designs that merge art and fashion, helping brands and individuals express their unique identities.
